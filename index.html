A Wedding by the Water

A brief ceremony and exchange of vows will be followed by an evening of jovial fellowship with friends and family by the water of the Puget Sound.


We'll gather at 5:30 pm, and the ceremony will begin shortly thereafter.


Our color palette and theme is directly inspired by the films of Wes Anderson.

Wayfinding

Port Orchard can be reached from West Seattle via the Fauntleroy-Southworth Ferry or from Downtown Seattle via the Seattle-Bremerton Ferry.


Links to ferry schedules will be updated as the date draws closer.


You can skip the ferry entirely by driving I-5 South and WA-16 West to reach Port Orchard.

Miscellaneous

Please dress relaxed formal. Feel fancy, but comfortable. Bonus points for refences to the theme (see above).


Weather for Port Orchard in late June is most often partly cloudy with highs in the low 70's and lows in the 50's. Summertime rain in Puget Sound is less likely, but always possible.


Free parking is available across the street from the celebration. Signs will direct you as you arrive.


While there will be no designated dance floor, there will be music, and you are encouraged to respond if the spirit moves you.


If you notice a celebrity or you are one, please exercise respectful discretion.


There will be dogs.
